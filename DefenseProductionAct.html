What is the Defense Production Act and How does it apply to the COVID19 Crisis?

updated April 4, 2020

To address potential supply shortages, President Trump has begun to invoke the "Defense Production Act". This is a 1950 law that gives the federal government more control over industrial production. However, its specific implementation can vary. There are early indications that Trump is hesitant to apply it widely, and there are concerns that any increase in production may not get to states in time to meet vital needs.
