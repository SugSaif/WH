Mass Media Disinformation

Major purveyors of information that is false and misleading include many broadcasters on AM-talk radio, Fox New, Sinclair media, and OANN. Most of us are familar with these networks and what they do, but for a very good summary of both their impact and some of the history of how these networks developed, a 'must-see' movie is 'The Brainwashing of my Dad', which can be viewed online for free (depending on your providers) or for a small fee.

Through interviews with "media luminaries, cognitive linguists, grassroots activist groups such as: Noam Chomsky, Steve Rendall, Jeff Cohen, Eric Boehlert, George Lakoff, Carol Wallin from STOP RUSH, HearYourselfThink, Claire Conner and others, “Brainwashing” unravels the plan to shift the country to the Right over the last 30 years, largely through media manipulation. The result has led to fewer voices, less diversity of opinion, massive intentional misinformation and greater division of our country." Stealth

There are now, unfortunately, many stealth right-wing networks. By 'stealth' what is meant here is that they do many of their activities behind the scenes, exert a tremendous amount of influence without much public comment, maintain 'secretive' activites over a very long time (decades), and often receive large amounts of funding from 'dark' sources whose interests they serve and protect. Not everything they do is in secret. They usually have very specific tactics that are public and often highly effective. Three of the most important are the Koch network, the Council for National Policy, and the Federalist Society.
