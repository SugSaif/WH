Zimbardo's Prison Experiment

In 1971, Stanford University professor Dr. Philip Zimbardo cast a group of student volunteers as prisoners and guards in a simulated jail. The results were disturbing, as the students took their roles very quickly, and in particular the students simulating guards became sadistic and abusive in a matter of days.
